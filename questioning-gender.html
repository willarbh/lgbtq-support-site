Exploring Your Gender Identity

Gender identity is your internal sense of your gender, which may or may not match your assigned sex at birth. Gender exists on a spectrum beyond just male or female.

Helpful Terms

  • Transgender (Trans): When your gender identity differs from assigned sex.
  • Nonbinary: A gender identity outside the male/female binary.
  • Genderqueer: Rejecting traditional gender categories.
  • Genderfluid: Moving between genders over time.
  • Coming Out: Sharing your gender identity when ready.
