Exploring Your Sexual Orientation

Sexual orientation is about who you feel emotionally, romantically, or physically attracted to. You might identify as gay, lesbian, bisexual, pansexual, queer, asexual, or none of these — and that’s okay. Your feelings may change over time, and there’s no rush to choose a label.

Helpful Terms

  • Bisexual: Attraction to more than one gender.
  • Pansexual: Attraction regardless of gender.
  • Queer: A broad term for sexual orientations outside heteronormativity.
  • Asexual: Little or no sexual attraction to others, though romantic attraction may still exist.
  • Questioning: Exploring your sexual orientation without fully identifying yet.
